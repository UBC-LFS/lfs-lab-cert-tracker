campaign About LFS Training Record Management System

The LFS Training Record Management System (TRMS) aims to collect and keep track of the training records required for all students, staffs and faculties that are working in LFS. This system will ensure that people working in specific space or area in LFS receive the appropriate training catered to the work environment.
